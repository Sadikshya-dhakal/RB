About PahiloKhana

Your ultimate destination for discovering, sharing, and enjoying authentic Nepali recipes from across the regions.

Our Mission

We believe that cooking should be accessible, enjoyable, and inspiring for everyone. PahiloKhana connects home cooks, professional chefs, and food enthusiasts from across Nepal and around the globe.

Whether you're a beginner learning to make Dal Bhat or an experienced chef perfecting Newari delicacies, PahiloKhana provides you with authentic recipes, cultural insights, and a supportive community to create amazing meals every day.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, PahiloKhana started as a passion project by Nepali food lovers who wanted to preserve and share our rich culinary heritage. We noticed that many traditional recipes were being lost as younger generations moved away from home cooking.

Today, we've grown into a vibrant community where people share their family recipes, learn authentic cooking techniques, and celebrate the diversity of Nepali cuisine from the mountains to the plains.
