Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my own recipe?

Simply create an account and click on "Add Recipe" in your dashboard. Follow the step-by-step guide to share your delicious creations with our community!

Is the platform free to use?

Yes! Basic features are completely free. We also offer premium features with a subscription for enhanced experience and exclusive content.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Absolutely! Click the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your favorites. You can access them anytime from your profile.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the report button on any recipe or comment. You can also contact us directly through this form. We take all reports seriously.

Can I advertise on PahiloKhana?

Yes! We offer various advertising opportunities. Please email business@pahilokhana.com for partnership inquiries.

How can I contribute to the blog?

We love guest contributors! Send us your food stories, cooking tips, or cultural insights via the contact form above.
